About Nandonik


Based in the vibrant city of Kolkata, Nandonik is a licensed filmmaking company passionate about storytelling that is both raw and deeply authentic. With a distinct, rustic approach to film, we craft stories that resonate on a global scale, earning us a devoted community of over 1 million followers across Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and Discord.

Our films break boundaries, speaking to the heart and celebrating life in its most unfiltered form. Honored with numerous national and international awards, Nandonik is recognized for its cinematic excellence and innovative artistry. Every project we undertake is a journey where vision meets grit, and we are proud to share these journeys with you.

Thank you for being a part of our creative world!
